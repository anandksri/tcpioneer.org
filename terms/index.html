Important information about TC Pioneer

At TC Pioneer, we are committed to creating a safe, ethical, and supportive environment for all our members. Our community's purpose is strictly educational, and we focus on promoting knowledge, skill development, and responsible cybersecurity practices.

By joining TC Pioneer, you agree to abide by the following terms and guidelines:

1. Educational Purpose Only

The activities, discussions, and resources within TC Pioneer community are intended solely for educational purposes. We focus on ethical hacking, cybersecurity education, and responsible practices aligned with the law.

2. No Support for Illegal Activities

We have a zero-tolerance policy for illegal activities including hacking, breaches, cyberattacks, phishing, or unethical behavior. Any such activity will lead to immediate removal from the community.

3. Personal Responsibility

Members are solely responsible for their actions. TC Pioneer does not provide protection, legal assistance, or cover for unlawful behavior.

4. Ethical Standards

Members must act with integrity, honesty, and respect. Any action that harms individuals, organizations, or violates privacy will not be tolerated.

5. Monitoring & Consequences

Activities may be monitored. Violations can lead to permanent removal and may be reported to relevant authorities when necessary.

6. Respect & Collaboration

Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior is not allowed. We aim to maintain a safe and inclusive learning environment.

7. Third-Party Responsibility

We are not responsible for third-party tools, services, or links shared by members. Use them at your own risk.

8. Copyright & Intellectual Property

Members must respect copyright laws and avoid sharing pirated or unauthorized content.

9. Community Engagement

Sharing knowledge is encouraged — misuse of information for harm is strictly prohibited.

10. Limitation of Liability

TC Pioneer is not responsible for damages, losses, or consequences caused by member actions.

11. Changes to This Disclaimer

We may update these terms anytime. Continued participation implies acceptance.

By being a part of TC Pioneer, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to these terms. Our goal is to build a safe, ethical, and empowering cybersecurity community.
