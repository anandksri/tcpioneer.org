Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at TC Pioneer

At TC Pioneer, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, services, and community.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic personal information such as your name, email address, social handles, and other details you voluntarily provide when joining our community or contacting us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for communication, community updates, learning resources, and improving our services. We do not sell or misuse your data.

3. Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and optimize our platform.

5. Third-Party Services

Some links or tools may redirect you to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

6. Community Safety

We never encourage sharing sensitive personal data publicly. Members are responsible for protecting their own privacy.

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or removal of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of our platform implies acceptance of changes.

By using TC Pioneer platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We aim to maintain a secure, transparent, and trustworthy environment for all members.
