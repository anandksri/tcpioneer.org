Our Mission

Building a safer
digital generation

We are proud to be the first to introduce seminars with live hacking demonstrations in Nepal, setting a new standard for cybersecurity education.

Our mission is to empower students with real-world knowledge, hands-on experience, and the mindset needed to defend and build responsibly in the digital world.

About Us

Who we are
and why we exist

TC Pioneer is a student-driven cybersecurity initiative focused on practical learning, real-world examples, and community growth.

We believe in learning by doing. Our programs include live attack demonstrations, technical workshops, CTF events, and cyber awareness campaigns.

Our community is open to everyone — from beginners to advanced learners — building a digitally secure generation together.
